# LIVIE CAPITAL ANTI-GOALS

## Motto

Protect. Compound. Grow.

## Purpose

This document defines what Livie Capital refuses to become. Growth is only valuable when it is built on discipline, transparency, and responsible capital management.

## What Livie Capital Refuses

### No Unrealistic Return Promises

Livie Capital will not promise guaranteed returns, exaggerated performance, or outcomes that cannot be supported by disciplined process and transparent risk disclosure.

### No Gambling Mentality

Livie Capital is not a speculation engine. Every investment decision must be grounded in risk assessment, position sizing, capital allocation discipline, and a defined investment rationale.

### No Hidden Losses

Losses must be measured, reported, and addressed. Livie Capital will not conceal drawdowns, unrealized losses, execution mistakes, or strategy failures.

### No Growth at Any Cost

Livie Capital will not pursue assets, users, partnerships, or expansion if doing so compromises capital preservation, trust, or risk discipline.

### No Misleading Marketing

Livie Capital will not use performance claims, selective data, vague language, or emotional persuasion to create a false impression of safety, certainty, or superiority.

### No Excessive Leverage

Leverage must never threaten the survival of the portfolio. Livie Capital will not use excessive leverage to chase returns, recover losses, or create artificial growth.

### No Abandoning Risk Management Rules

Risk rules exist to protect the firm, its capital, and its long-term mission. Livie Capital will not suspend risk discipline for convenience, pressure, fear, or greed.

## Standard

If an action does not protect capital, improve responsible compounding, or support sustainable growth, it does not belong inside Livie Capital.
