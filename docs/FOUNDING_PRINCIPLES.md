# LIVIE CAPITAL

## Motto
Protect. Compound. Grow.

## Mission

Preserve and grow capital through disciplined risk management, intelligent capital allocation, and long-term thinking.

## Vision

Build a global investment firm that creates opportunity through transparency, education, and responsible capital management.

## Investment Objective

Generate approximately 17% annualized net returns over the long term while prioritizing capital preservation and consistency over return maximization.

Livie Capital's base reporting currency is USD.

## Core Belief

The best way to create wealth is to protect it.

## Investment Universe

- S&P 500 ETFs
- Nasdaq ETFs
- Technology Equities
- Value Equities
- Dividend Equities
- Gold (XAUUSD)
- GBPUSD

## Risk Framework

- Maximum risk per trade: 0.80%
- Maximum portfolio drawdown: 20%
- Mandatory strategy review: 15% decline from equity peak
- New positions suspended if maximum drawdown threshold is reached

## Priority Hierarchy

1. Capital Preservation
2. Consistency
3. Growth

## Market Philosophy

Livie Capital does not attempt to predict markets. It seeks to allocate capital where risk-adjusted opportunities are most attractive.

## Community Commitment

- Financial Education
- Community Development
- Investor Growth
- Opportunity Creation

## Long-Term Goal

To evolve into a global capital management firm trusted by individuals, families, and institutions.
