# FOUNDER LETTER 2026

## LIVIE CAPITAL

To our future partners, clients, and community,

Livie Capital was created with a simple belief: capital should be managed with patience, discipline, and respect. In a world that often celebrates speed, speculation, and attention, we believe the enduring value is found in thoughtful decisions repeated over long periods of time.

The purpose of Livie Capital is not to chase excitement. It is to protect capital, compound it responsibly, and use that growth to create opportunity. Our motto is simple because the work itself must remain simple:

Protect. Compound. Grow.

## Why Livie Capital Was Created

Livie Capital was created to build an investment firm rooted in trust, clarity, and long-term thinking. Many people are exposed to markets through noise, promises, and short-term emotion. We believe capital deserves a better environment.

The firm exists to bring institutional discipline to capital management while remaining understandable, transparent, and aligned with the people it serves. Our objective is not to appear sophisticated for its own sake. Our objective is to make sound decisions, manage risk honestly, and build durable wealth over time.

## Why Protecting Capital Matters

The first rule of compounding is survival. A portfolio that suffers permanent impairment loses more than money. It loses time, flexibility, confidence, and opportunity.

Protecting capital does not mean avoiding all risk. Risk is part of investing. But risk must be sized, measured, and respected. Every position must have a reason. Every loss must be acknowledged. Every drawdown must teach us something.

The best way to create wealth is to protect it.

## Why Consistency Matters More Than Excitement

Markets will always offer excitement. There will always be a new story, a new asset, a new prediction, and a new reason to abandon discipline. Livie Capital is not built for that cycle.

We would rather earn consistent returns with controlled risk than pursue spectacular outcomes that rely on luck, leverage, or optimism. A steady process may look ordinary in the short term, but over time, consistency becomes extraordinary.

The goal is not to win every day. The goal is to remain rational every day.

## The Long-Term Vision

Our long-term vision is to build Livie Capital into a global investment firm trusted by individuals, families, and institutions. That will not happen through marketing alone. It will happen through process, transparency, performance, and time.

We intend to build the firm carefully. We will grow only when growth strengthens the foundation. We will avoid shortcuts that compromise risk management, trust, or culture. We will measure progress not only by assets under management, but by the quality of our decisions and the durability of our relationships.

## Education, Community, and Opportunity

Livie Capital must be more than a portfolio. It must be a platform for education, community development, investor growth, and opportunity creation.

Financial knowledge can change lives. Access to better information, better habits, and better capital management can help individuals and communities make stronger decisions. We believe that as Livie Capital grows, its responsibility grows with it.

Our commitment is to share what we learn, operate transparently, and help create a culture where investing is understood as stewardship rather than speculation.

## Closing

The work ahead will require patience. There will be difficult markets, mistakes, and periods where discipline feels slow. But we believe that thoughtful capital management, practiced consistently, can build something lasting.

Livie Capital begins with a simple promise:

We will protect capital first.
We will compound responsibly.
We will grow with discipline.

That is the standard. That is the path.

Founder, Livie Capital

2026
