# LIVIE CAPITAL NORTH STAR

## Motto

Protect. Compound. Grow.

## Purpose

Every decision made by Livie Capital must contribute to one of three outcomes:

1. Protect capital.
2. Compound capital responsibly.
3. Create sustainable growth.

## Decision Filter

Before implementing any feature, investment strategy, partnership, or business initiative, ask:

* Does this protect capital?
* Does this improve compounding?
* Does this support sustainable growth?

If the answer is no to all three, it does not belong inside Livie Capital.

## Principles

* Performance matters.
* Discipline is mandatory.
* Trust is earned.
* Growth is the result.

## Long-Term Vision

Build a global capital management firm that combines institutional discipline, radical transparency, and community-driven opportunity creation.
